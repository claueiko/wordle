How to play

You have 6 tries to guess the WORDLE.

Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.

You'll be using your keyboard. Hit the ENTER button to submit.

After each guess, the color of the titles will change to show how close your guess was to the word. If it turns green, the letter is in the correct spot.
If it turns yellow, the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
And if it turns grey, the letter is not in the word in any spot.

Points:
100 points if you guess at first.
80 points if you guess on your second try.
60 points on third try.
40 points for the fourth.
20 points for the fifth.
10 points if you guess on your last attempt!
